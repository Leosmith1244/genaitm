About Gen AI Speakers Toastmasters

Developing ethical AI leaders through confident communication

Our Core Algorithm

Founded in 2025, Gen AI Speakers Toastmasters is a specialized District 106 club for AI professionals and enthusiasts to develop their communication skills in a technical context.

Our Mission

To provide a supportive environment where AI professionals develop communication and leadership skills through technology-themed meetings.

Our Vision

A world where every AI professional can communicate their ideas as effectively as they build their algorithms.

Our Values

Constructive feedback, ethical communication, and a welcoming environment for all AI enthusiasts.

Our Meeting Protocol

We meet weekly with a focus on technical communication development:

1

Virtual Meetings

Weekly Toastmasters meetings with AI-themed roles and evaluations, held via Zoom every Friday at 12 PM Central.

2

Technical Focus

Special emphasis on communicating complex AI concepts to both technical and non-technical audiences.

Accessible Format

All meetings are fully virtual to accommodate our global membership of AI professionals.

Our Specialized Roles
